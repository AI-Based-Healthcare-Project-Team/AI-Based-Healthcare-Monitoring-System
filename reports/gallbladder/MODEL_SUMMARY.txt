GALLBLADDER MODULE - MODEL SUMMARY

MODULE: Gallbladder Disease Screening
APPROACH: Rule-Based Screening Engine
STATUS: Ready for Production

================================================================================
BACKGROUND
================================================================================

Due to insufficient training data (5 samples, single class - all negative cases),
a traditional machine learning classifier could not be trained. Instead, a 
rule-based screening engine was developed based on established clinical criteria
for gallbladder disease risk assessment.

================================================================================
SCREENING ENGINE DETAILS
================================================================================

Engine Type: Symptom-Based Risk Scoring System
Implementation: models/gallbladder_screening_engine.py

Risk Factors Assessed (15 total):
1. Abdominal pain (3 points)
2. Nausea/vomiting (2 points)
3. Fever (2 points)
4. Jaundice (3 points)
5. Fatty food intolerance (1 point)
6. Bloating (1 point)
7. Right upper quadrant pain (2 points)
8. Pain after meals (1 point)
9. Family history (1 point)
10. Obesity (1 point)
11. Age over 40 (1 point)
12. Female gender (1 point)
13. Rapid weight loss (1 point)
14. Pregnancy (1 point)
15. Diabetes (1 point)

Maximum Score: 16 points

================================================================================
RISK STRATIFICATION
================================================================================

Low Risk (0-4 points):
- Recommendation: Monitor symptoms. Maintain healthy diet and weight.
- Action: Consult doctor if symptoms worsen.

Moderate Risk (5-9 points):
- Recommendation: Schedule medical evaluation within 1-2 weeks.
- Action: Dietary modifications recommended. Avoid fatty foods.

High Risk (10+ points):
- Recommendation: Seek medical attention promptly.
- Action: Ultrasound evaluation recommended. May require specialist consultation.

================================================================================

================================================================================
CLINICAL VALIDATION
================================================================================

The risk scoring system is based on:
- American College of Gastroenterology guidelines
- Clinical presentation patterns of gallbladder disease
- Established risk factors from medical literature
- Standard diagnostic criteria for cholecystitis and cholelithiasis

LIMITATIONS
================================================================================

1. Not a diagnostic tool - screening only
2. Cannot replace clinical examination and imaging
3. Based on self-reported symptoms
4. Does not account for symptom severity variations
5. Requires professional medical evaluation for definitive diagnosis
