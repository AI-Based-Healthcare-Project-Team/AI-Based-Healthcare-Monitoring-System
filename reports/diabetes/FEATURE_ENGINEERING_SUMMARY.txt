FEATURE ENGINEERING SUMMARY
============================================================

Engineered Features:
------------------------------------------------------------

1. GLUCOSE METRICS:
   - Avg_Blood_Glucose: Mean glucose level per patient
   - Min_Blood_Glucose: Minimum glucose level
   - Max_Blood_Glucose: Maximum glucose level
   - Std_Blood_Glucose: Standard deviation of glucose
   - Median_Blood_Glucose: Median glucose level
   - Glucose_Variability: Coefficient of variation (std/mean)

2. INSULIN METRICS:
   - Avg_Regular_Insulin: Average regular insulin dose
   - Avg_NPH_Insulin: Average NPH insulin dose
   - Total_Daily_Insulin: Average total daily insulin

3. MONITORING METRICS:
   - Num_Glucose_Measurements: Total glucose measurements
   - Num_Insulin_Doses: Total insulin doses
   - Days_Monitored: Duration of monitoring period
   - Num_Hypoglycemic_Events: Count of hypoglycemic events

4. CLINICAL RISK INDICATORS:
   - Hyperglycemia_Percentage: % of readings > 200 mg/dl
   - Hypoglycemia_Percentage: % of readings < 70 mg/dl
   - Target_Range_Percentage: % of readings in 70-180 mg/dl

============================================================
Feature Engineering Techniques Applied:
------------------------------------------------------------
1. Aggregation: Patient-level statistics from time-series data
2. Derived Features: Variability and percentage metrics
3. Clinical Thresholds: Risk categorization based on medical guidelines
4. Temporal Features: Monitoring duration and frequency
